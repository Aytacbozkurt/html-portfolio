The Best Movies According to Aytac

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Shawshank Redemption

It was inspiring that the character in the movie overcame the difficulties and injustices in his life with passion and dedication by never giving up.

The Matrix

The feeling of opening the door to a new world in the first movie of the series was exciting.

Life Is Beautiful

A father how to make his child joyful and peaceful even in the most difficult situation is described in the best way.
